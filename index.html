Tony Hawk Foundation Raises Big Money At Celeb Fundraiser

October 10, 2013

It was just over 90 degrees in Los Angeles, but that didn't stop the throngs from coming out to celebrate the 10th Annual Tony Hawk's Stands Up Skateparks Benefit Presented by SLS Hotel & Casino Las Vegas on Saturday at the Green Acre Estate in Beverly Hills.
